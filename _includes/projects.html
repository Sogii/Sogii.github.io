How do you manage the endlessly possible variations of different variables and opinions? And how do you visualize the impact and compromises of those ideas intuitively? Together with Liz van der Jagt, Ruben van den Bosch, Tim Kolen and Margot Galama, we organized an exhibition about the Dommel, a river flowing through the heart of Eindhoven.
